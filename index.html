Services:
While watching your home and taking care of your furchildren, I will love them like they are my own. I will spend all day showering them with affection and follow any instructions you have for their care (if any). I have 25+ years of animal handling. Types of animals I have experience with are dogs, cats, caged rodents, fish, and reptiles.
Are you not able to travel with your children or would you like an adult only vacation? I can also take care of your children for you so you don’t need to worry about additional child care. I have over 20 years child care experience as well as raising my own children. I have a diploma in Early Childhood Education and up to date First Aid training.

During my time in your home while taking care of the beings you treasure more than anything, I will also provide light housekeeping and yard care if needed.
